Cool stuff About Cats

Fun facts about cats:

  • cats can see 6-8 times better than we can in the dark but lack true night vision
  • most cats are naturally lactose intolerant
  • Cats can't go back down a tree head first due to their claws.
  • Cats have 230 bones compared to our 206, and 32 muscles per ear for pinpointing sounds.

Stuff cats hate

loud noises
cats have sensitive hearing so vacuum cleaners ,fire works or shouting
Water
Most cats dislike being wet, though some breeds (like Bengals or Turkish Vans) may tolerate or even enjoy it
Strong Smells (Citrus, Vinegar, etc.)
Cats have a powerful sense of smell, so strong scents like citrus, mint, or cleaning chemicals can be unpleasant
Sticky or Uncomfortable Surfaces
Things like tape, sticky paws, or certain textures (like aluminum foil) can annoy them.

Stuff cats love

Catnip & Silver Vine
Many cats go crazy for catnip (nepetalactone), while others prefer silver vine (even stronger for some cats!). It triggers a euphoric, playful reaction.
Interactive Toys (Feather Wands, Laser Pointers)
Cats love hunting-like play
  • Feather wands (mimics birds)
  • Laser pointers (trigger chase instinct)
  • Crinkly balls (sound + movement)
Boxes & Cozy Hideaways
  • Cardboard boxes = instant cat magnets (hiding, napping, ambushing)
  • Warm spots (sunbeams, heated beds, laundry piles).
Chin & Cheek Scratches
They have scent glands there, so gentle scratches make them purr and knead in bliss.
